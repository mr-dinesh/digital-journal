### Prompt Used

 -First Iteration: Create a summary of the podcast Think Fast, TalkSmart by Matt Abrahms, I want to extract a specific part of each podcast episode, what is the advice which each podcast episode guest shares, which can be put on a powerpoint slide and is 7 words or less.

 -Second Iteration: Add natural, inspiring music. Also add slow fade out and transitions between slides. Where possible add some washed out imagery to make the text more relevant and appropriate.

### Outcome - Version1: 
- 🎬 Think Fast, Talk Smart — Episode Montage

- 5.6 minutes long · 1280×720 HD · 30 FPS
- 165 key episodes covered, starting from EP 1 all the way to EP 270
- Smooth cross-fade transitions between every card
- Category-colour coded — each episode's accent colour (teal for Communication, red for Conflict, purple for Mindset, gold for Feedback, etc.) matches its topic
- Progress bar at the bottom so you always know how far through the series you are

- 🎵 Music — fully synthesised motivational background track built from scratch:

- Uplifting Cmaj7 → Am7 → Fmaj7 → G chord progression
- Piano-like tones with harmonic overtones + a soft pad layer
- Subtle kick + hi-hat rhythm at 100 BPM
- Fades in and out with the video

### Structure:

- Title card — "165 Episodes · Core Advice · 7 Words or Less"
- Episode cards — each showing the EP number, category tag, guest name, and the advice in large bold serif type
- Outro card — "Listen. Learn. Communicate."on1

### Version2 
- ✅ Video v2 is ready — 1280×720 HD · 6.1 minutes · 21.6 MB

- What was improved:
- 🎵 Music — fully rebuilt from scratch:

- Karplus-Strong plucked string algorithm — the same physics-based synthesis used in professional samplers, which naturally models how a real guitar/harp string decays
- String pad layer — 5 detuned sine oscillators with slow attack/release for lush warmth
- Choir pad layer — formant-weighted harmonics with vibrato for that orchestral "ahh" quality
- Hall reverb — multi-tap delay network mimicking a concert hall at 72ms room size
- Swelling dynamics — the track rises gently from silence, peaks in the middle, and fades out naturally at the end
- Tempo: 72 BPM on an uplifting Cmaj9 → Fmaj9 → Am9 → Gsus4 loop

- 🖼️ Washed-out background art — unique per category:

- Listening/Communication/Clarity → concentric sound-wave rings expanding from centre
- Technology/Innovation → circuit-board grid with glowing nodes and trace lines
- Leadership/Performance → layered mountain silhouette peaks
- Mindset/Neuroscience/Behavior → neural network of connected dots and edges
- Conflict/Negotiation → diagonal tension lines crossing from both sides
- Storytelling/Authenticity → sweeping open-book page curves
- Body Language/Persuasion → abstract human figure outline
- Humor/Wellbeing → soft sine-wave undulations
- Culture/Teamwork/Meeting → interconnected circle nodes in a ring

- ✨ Transitions:

- 0.55s cubic ease-in-out cross-fade between every episode
- Longer 1.1s fade on the final transition into the outro
- Title card holds 3.5s; outro holds 4.5s

### Takeaways

 - Narrative needs to be added, to stitch together all of the advice. Would be more "consumable", instead of just staring at bullet points on the 200+ slides
 
 - The music sucks. Tried to improve it, but need something more bespoke and relevant to the content.
 
 - A human voice reading it out, would probably improve the product.
